Catering Services

We offer three different catering services:


Taco Bar

Our taco bar is a delicious and affordable way to feed a crowd. We'll bring everything you need to make your own tacos, including your choice of meat, fresh toppings, and soft tortillas.

Burrito Bar

Our burrito bar is perfect for a casual gathering. We'll provide all the fixings to make your own burritos, including your choice of meat, rice, beans, cheese, and salsa.

Chips & Salsa Bar

Our chips and salsa bar is a great way to start any party. We'll provide a variety of salsas, including mild, medium, and hot, along with a basket of freshly air-fried tortilla chips.
