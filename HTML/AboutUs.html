Our History

Taqueria Los Amigos was founded in 2010 by two childhood friends, Juan and Carlos. They had always dreamed of opening their own restaurant, and after many years of hard work and saving, they were finally able to make it a reality. Their goal was to share their love of Mexican food with their community, and to provide a place where people could come together to enjoy good food and good company.
